About Naye Pankh

We build sustainable change with student-led programs that support children, women and vulnerable communities.

Mission

Empower underprivileged families with education, nutrition and dignity so every dream gets the chance to take flight.

Vision

A compassionate India where every child learns, every family eats, and every community thrives.

Values

Accountability, empathy and measurable impact guide every program and donation.

Our Programs

High-impact campaigns across education, nutrition, hygiene and animal welfare.

Education Support

Study kits, scholarships and mentoring for children from low-income communities.

Food Distribution

Nutritious meals delivered to families during festivals, lockdowns and relief drives.

Women Hygiene

Hygiene kits, health camps and menstrual education for girls and women.

Animal Welfare

Rescue, medical care and rehabilitation for stray animals across rural communities.
