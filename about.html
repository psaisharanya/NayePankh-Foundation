About Naye Pankh

Think global, Act local. Naye Pankh Foundation is a student-led NGO working across multiple cities to create sustainable change through education, health and food distribution. We are registered under UP Government and have 80G & 12A certifications.

Our Mission

To uplift underprivileged communities by providing education, food, hygiene and health support — creating resilient communities and brighter futures.
